What Does OAO Do?

OAO'S Misson

At OAO,we strive to bring awareness to people about orphans. We carry out mass education to people and also offer private workshops and training to those who are intrested

Orphans are a very vunerable group of children whose parents are no longer alive. In most cases, after the demise of the parents,they are left to suffer on their own and are not able to get basic needs.

Most street children are orphans

In 2020,following the Covid-19 pandemic,the government started a foundation where orphans could stay. However, that has not been the case as the streets of Nairobi are still filled with them
